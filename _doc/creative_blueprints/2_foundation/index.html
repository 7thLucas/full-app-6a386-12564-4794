Foundation

The Problem

Existing social platforms are restrictive and charge premium ad rates that only established brands can afford — locking upcoming entrepreneurs out of their own audience.

  • ✕ Ad costs are prohibitive for early-stage founders
  • ✕ Platform policies restrict small business visibility
  • ✕ The playing field is tilted toward big-budget brands

The Solution

A social network built for entrepreneurs — affordable advertising, open access, and a fair platform where upcoming business owners compete on the quality of their ideas, not the size of their budget.

Affordable audience reach

Ad tools priced for early-stage founders — reach your audience without the enterprise price tag.

Fewer restrictions, more freedom

Platform policies designed to help entrepreneurs grow — not gatekeep them from their own community.
