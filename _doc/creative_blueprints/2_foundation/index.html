Foundation

The Problem

Existing social platforms are restrictive and charge premium ad rates that only established brands can afford — locking upcoming entrepreneurs out of their own audience.

  • ✕ Ad costs are prohibitive for early-stage founders
  • ✕ Platform policies restrict small business visibility
  • ✕ The playing field is tilted toward big-budget brands

The Solution

A social network platform purpose-built around the simplest, stickiest loop: post content, follow the people you care about, engage meaningfully. No algorithmic noise — just the connections you chose.

Frictionless onboarding

New users reach their first meaningful interaction in under 60 seconds.

Retention by design

Feed quality and notification relevance are prioritised over volume — every signal earns its place.
