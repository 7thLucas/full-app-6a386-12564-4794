Core Features

User Profiles

Personalised profiles with bio, avatar, and curated post history to express identity.

A colorful, inviting feed with placeholder cards so it never feels empty — topped by a right-to-left scrolling profile strip that surfaces people to follow at a glance.

Follow & Connect

Follow users or topics to curate the feed; build a network of intentional connections at your pace.

Content Creation

Compose text posts, share links or media — fast, frictionless creation at the heart of the loop.

Notifications

Timely alerts for new followers, reactions, and replies — surfaced by relevance, never noise.

Engagement

React to and comment on posts; direct, lightweight engagement that keeps the conversation alive.
