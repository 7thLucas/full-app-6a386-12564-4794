# SpinCity — Product Overview

## What It Is
SpinCity is a social network platform built for early-stage entrepreneurs, small business owners, and early-stage founders who are priced out of advertising on existing platforms. It is also open to creators, freelancers, and general users who want a fairer, less restrictive social network.

## Problem Being Solved
Existing social platforms are restrictive and expensive for advertising — inaccessible and unfair to upcoming entrepreneurs. Small business owners and founders cannot afford the high ad rates that major platforms charge, and platform policies often work against them.

## Core Value Proposition
An affordable, open social network that gives entrepreneurs fair access to audience-building tools — lower ad costs, fewer restrictions, and a platform designed with the emerging business owner in mind.

## Tagline
Find your home. Build your hustle.

## Day-One MVP Feature: The Inviting Home Feed
- A clean, colorful, visually vibrant social feed designed to make every new user feel like they've found a community from first scroll
- Placeholder content cards pre-populated on first load so the feed never feels empty or cold to a new arrival
- A horizontal scrolling profile-picture strip flowing RIGHT TO LEFT — surfacing people to follow at a glance, above the main feed
- Core social loop: post, follow, engage

## Positioning & Tone
- Warm, colorful, inviting — feels like home from the first scroll
- Differentiator: first-impression feed signals belonging before a single follow is made

## Strategic Principles
- Start with the simplest social loop that delivers real value (post, follow, engage)
- Design for retention from day one — feed quality over volume
- Onboarding frictionless: new users reach first meaningful interaction in under 60 seconds
